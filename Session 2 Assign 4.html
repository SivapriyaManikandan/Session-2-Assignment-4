GOPALAN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

GOPALAN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL was established in 2004 by the Gopalan Group, which has a long standing reputation of sincerity and commitment in the field of Property Development, Architecture and Organic Farming. The endeavor of reviving the age-old concept of education in a new child-friendly environment is spearheaded by a dedicated group who believe, "Helping young minds to realize their full potential is a creative challenge."
